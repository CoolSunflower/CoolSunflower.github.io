## Overview

A deep learning project for the InterIIT Tech Meet that achieves unprecedented spatial resolution of lunar surface elemental composition data.

## Technical Approach

### Graph Neural Networks
Leveraged spatial relationships between measurement points using message-passing GNN architectures.

### Dynamic Optimization Pipeline
Developed online optimization using Lagrangian and non-convex optimization heuristics to improve accuracy.

### Chandrayaan-2 Data
Processed CLASS X-Ray Fluorescence data to extract elemental composition maps at subpixel resolution.

## Results

- Achieved resolution improvement from 12.5km × 100km to <2km × 2km
- Validated against ground truth measurements
- Presented at InterIIT Tech Meet
