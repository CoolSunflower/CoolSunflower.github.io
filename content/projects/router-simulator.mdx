## Overview

A comprehensive router simulator for studying and comparing different packet scheduling algorithms under various network conditions.

## Implemented Algorithms

### Priority Scheduling
Packets prioritized based on predefined rules and QoS requirements.

### Weighted Fair Queuing (WFQ)
Fair bandwidth allocation with configurable weights per flow.

### Round Robin (RR)
Simple time-sliced scheduling for baseline comparison.

### iSLIP
Advanced scheduling algorithm minimizing head-of-line (HOL) blocking.

## Benchmarking Results

Tested under multiple traffic patterns:
- Uniform traffic distribution
- Non-uniform (bursty) traffic
- Real-world trace replay

**Key Finding**: iSLIP proved most efficient for achieving fairness while minimizing HOL blocking.
